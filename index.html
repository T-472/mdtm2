Some Recommended Military Movies

Want Battle Realism Depicted on the Big Screen? Look No Further!

Top Gun: Maverick

The sequel to the first requies no prior knowledge to marvel at the aerial feats performed with real aircraft!

While there may be a number of inaccuracies in regards to total realism (thanks to numerous youtube breakdowns by prior military personnel), Maverick incorporates enough of it in conjunction with real aircraft to deliver high-octane jet fighter action, as to be expected from Mr. Cruise.

Saving Private Ryan

A brutal, visceral look into one of the bloodiest campaigns of WWII

The exploration of a tale not uncommonly found during this time period. The grisly and explicit details of war and comraderie are left intact and full to bear, all of which were and continue to be lauded as highly accurate to firsthand accounts. A hard watch that accomplishes its mission of depicting a hard time.

Das Boot (1981)

A cold, deep dive into the "other side"

A unique perspective on the opposing side during WWII. The Kriegsmarine, known for their infamous U-boats, were a terrifying presence in the open seas. However, tactics and strategies to combat them rose rapidly as the war progressed. Follow this German crew (in what can only be described as an epic, due to its length) through their accomplishments as well as their failures, and face humanity head-on as you come to know members as more than just "the enemy".
